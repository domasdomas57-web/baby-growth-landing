## F) Google Play full description outline

**Opening (value prop, ~300 chars)**  
Soriva is a calm baby tracker app for parents who want feeding, sleep, diapers, and growth in one place—not scattered notes and group chats. Log in seconds, sync with caregivers, and export clear summaries for pediatrician visits.

**Core features (bullets)**
- **Feeding tracker** — breastfeeding, bottle feeds, pumping; last feed and daily timeline  
- **Sleep tracker** — naps, night sleep, schedule view  
- **Diaper tracker** — wet, dirty, daily counts  
- **Growth tracker** — weight, height, head circumference with history  
- **Caregiver sync** — mom, dad, nanny, grandparents on one baby profile  
- **Doctor reports** — export feeds, sleep, diapers & growth for chosen dates  
- **Widgets** — quick logging from home screen  
- **AI insights** — supportive summaries only; not medical advice  

**Trust & safety paragraph**  
Built by a parent. Privacy-conscious baby tracking. Soriva does not provide medical advice and does not replace your pediatrician. AI insights are informational only.

**Platforms & pricing**  
Free to download on Android. Optional subscription for premium features; billing via Google Play.

**Closing CTA**  
Download Soriva and start a calmer baby log today.

**Keywords to weave naturally (do not stuff):** newborn tracker, infant, breastfeeding, nursing, bottle, pumping, babylog, milestone, parenting, tracker, sleep, diaper, routine, pediatrician.
